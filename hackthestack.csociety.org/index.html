What is Hack The Stack?
Hack the Stack is an event focused on learning about common security vulnerabilities and how they are exploited. The competition will range from web based hacks, to protocol attacks, and memory leaks. Teams can be up to 4 and will be given points for challenges completed. Winners will receive prizes!
What can I do there?
The challenges will be set up so hints will be available as time progresses or if everyone gets stuck. We will also go over the exploits and how to perform them at the end. Even if you aren't sure you'll be able to do something, you will come out learning something!
How do I get started?
RSVP with the link above, and we'll consider you part of the competition! If something changes please be sure to email us and let us know at mmolo@purdue.edu
What do I bring, and where?
All you need to do is bring your laptop, a charger, and yourself! The event takes place in Forney G124. On your laptop, you'll also need to some type of SSH client.
